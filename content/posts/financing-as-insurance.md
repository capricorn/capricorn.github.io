---
title: "Financing as Insurance"
date: 2021-07-05T10:31:32-05:00
tags: [finance]
---

Say you have limited savings, but discover you must make a sizeable purchase.
This is a purchase you could afford, but would significantly eat away at
your savings. In this situation, going broke is a possibility if you happen to
lose your job soon after.

One approach is to instead treat financing like insurance. In this case, treat
the interest as a premium paid. Instead of heavily reducing your bankroll,
a monthly payment is made over a longer period of time. Financing
lets you maintain cash on hand, which could be the difference between
making or missing rent. In the meantime, savings can be built at a slower rate.
